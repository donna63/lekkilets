Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum stay duration?

Our minimum stay duration is 2 nights for most apartments. However, during peak seasons or for specific luxury suites, we may require a minimum stay of 3-5 nights.

Do you offer airport pickup services?

Yes, we offer airport pickup services for an additional fee. Please let us know your flight details at least 48 hours in advance so we can arrange for your comfortable transfer.

Are pets allowed in the apartments?

We love pets, but to maintain our high standards of cleanliness and comfort for all guests, we generally do not allow pets. Exceptions may be made for service animals with proper documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Cancellations made 7 days or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days of check-in receive a 50% refund. No refunds for no-shows or early departures.
