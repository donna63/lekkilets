Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Lekkilet emerged from a simple vision: to provide exceptional short-term accommodation that combines luxury, comfort, and convenience in Lagos' most vibrant neighborhoods.

What started as a single apartment in Lekki Phase 1 has grown into a premium portfolio of carefully curated properties across Lagos' prime locations. Each property is selected and designed to meet the highest standards of quality and comfort.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of hundreds of guests, from business travelers to families on vacation, all seeking the perfect home away from home.
