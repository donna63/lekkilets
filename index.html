What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellation Time Refund Amount
More than 14 days before check-in Full refund (100%)
7-14 days before check-in 50% refund
Less than 7 days before check-in No refund (can reschedule)
Same day cancellation No refund

Note: Rescheduling is available up to 48 hours before check-in at no extra charge, subject to availability.

Are the apartments kid-friendly and pet-friendly?

Kid-Friendly

Yes! We welcome families with children and provide:

  • Child safety features (socket covers, corner guards)
  • High chairs and cribs available on request
  • Family-friendly neighborhoods
  • Play areas in select buildings

Pet Policy

We have a selective pet-friendly policy:

  • Small pets allowed (under 25lbs)
  • Pet fee: ₦5,000 per stay
  • Certain breeds restricted
  • Advance notice required

Important: Please inform us about children or pets during booking so we can prepare accordingly.
